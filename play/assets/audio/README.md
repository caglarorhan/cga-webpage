# Audio Assets for CyberGrid Assault

Place your audio files in these folders. All files should be in MP3 format.

## Folder Structure

### `/weapons/` - Weapon Sounds
- `pulse_pistol_shoot.mp3` - Energy zap sound
- `pulse_pistol_reload.mp3` - Quick reload
- `plasma_rifle_shoot.mp3` - Plasma burst
- `plasma_rifle_reload.mp3` - Magazine swap
- `scatter_gun_shoot.mp3` - Shotgun blast
- `scatter_gun_reload.mp3` - Shell loading
- `fusion_cannon_shoot.mp3` - Heavy blast
- `fusion_cannon_reload.mp3` - Power charge
- `empty_click.mp3` - Empty magazine click (shared)
- `weapon_switch.mp3` - Weapon swap sound

### `/player/` - Player Sounds
- `footstep_metal.mp3` - Metallic footstep
- `damage.mp3` - Taking damage grunt
- `death.mp3` - Death sound
- `respawn.mp3` - Respawn/teleport in
- `low_health.mp3` - Low health warning (looping)

### `/combat/` - Combat Sounds
- `impact_wall.mp3` - Bullet hitting wall
- `impact_player.mp3` - Bullet hitting player
- `headshot.mp3` - Critical hit indicator
- `kill_confirmed.mp3` - Kill notification ding

### `/abilities/` - Ability Sounds
- `cast.mp3` - Generic ability cast
- `ready.mp3` - Ability ready notification
- `explosion.mp3` - Grenade/rocket explosion
- `teleport.mp3` - Teleport/blink sound
- `shield_deploy.mp3` - Shield activation
- `emp_blast.mp3` - EMP discharge
- `smoke_grenade.mp3` - Smoke deploy

### `/game/` - Game State Sounds
- `round_start.mp3` - Round beginning
- `round_end.mp3` - Round over
- `match_win.mp3` - Victory fanfare
- `match_lose.mp3` - Defeat sound
- `countdown_tick.mp3` - Countdown beep
- `countdown_go.mp3` - "GO!" sound

### `/ui/` - UI Sounds
- `click.mp3` - Button click
- `hover.mp3` - Button hover
- `open.mp3` - Menu open
- `close.mp3` - Menu close
- `success.mp3` - Success notification
- `error.mp3` - Error notification

### `/music/` - Background Music
- `menu_theme.mp3` - Main menu music
- `gameplay_loop.mp3` - In-game background music
- `intense_loop.mp3` - Intense combat music
- `victory_theme.mp3` - Victory screen music
- `defeat_theme.mp3` - Defeat screen music

## Recommended Sources (Free, Royalty-Free)

1. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/sound-effects/
2. **Freesound** - https://freesound.org/
3. **Mixkit** - https://mixkit.co/free-sound-effects/
4. **OpenGameArt** - https://opengameart.org/

## Tips

- Keep sound files short (< 2 seconds for effects)
- Normalize volume levels
- Use MP3 format for best browser compatibility
- Aim for 128kbps quality (good balance of size/quality)
