## üéØ Game Overview

**CyberGrid Assault** is a fast-paced, top-down tactical shooter with MOBA-inspired hero abilities. Assemble your team, choose your hero, and engage in intense 4v4 battles across neon-lit arenas.

### Key Features

- **Hero-Based Combat:** Each hero has unique abilities and playstyles
- **Tactical Gunplay:** Master recoil, movement, and positioning
- **Economy System:** Earn credits to buy weapons each round
- **Team Strategy:** Coordinate with allies for maximum effectiveness
- **Dynamic Arenas:** Use cover, flanking routes, and vertical play

## üèÜ Game Modes

### ‚öîÔ∏è Neon Deathmatch

A classic team deathmatch where eliminations are everything.

- **Objective:** Eliminate enemy players
- **Win Condition:** First team to reach the kill target wins the round
- **Teams:** Attackers vs Defenders

### üíæ Data Spike Assault

A tactical bomb-defusal mode inspired by classic tactical shooters.

- **Attackers:** Plant the Data Spike at designated upload points
- **Defenders:** Prevent the spike plant or defuse it
- **Win Conditions:**
  - Attackers: Successfully upload the Data Spike
  - Defenders: Eliminate all attackers OR defuse the spike

### Round Structure

- Matches are played as **Best of X** (configurable: 3, 5, 7, 9, or 13 rounds)
- First team to win the majority of rounds wins the match
- Teams switch sides at halftime
- **10-second countdown** between rounds for preparation

## ‚öôÔ∏è Game Mechanics

### Health & Shields

- **Health:** Your main HP pool. When it hits 0, you're eliminated.
- **Shields:** Absorbs damage before health. Some heroes have natural shields.
- **Regeneration:** Only Mira has passive health regen (out of combat).

### Movement

- **Base Speed:** 250 units/second (modified by hero speed multiplier)
- **Strafing:** Full speed in all directions
- **No Sprinting:** Consistent movement speed at all times

### Combat

- **Hitscan Weapons:** Instant bullet travel (no travel time)
- **Headshots:** 2x damage multiplier
- **Damage Falloff:** Some weapons deal less damage at range
- **Armor:** Reduces incoming damage by a percentage

### Abilities

- **Cooldowns:** Abilities have individual cooldown timers
- **Charges:** Some abilities have multiple charges (e.g., Katana's Dash Slash)
- **Ultimates:** Build ultimate charge through combat and objective play

## üí° Tips & Strategies

### General Tips

1. **Use Cover:** Peek, shoot, retreat. Don't stand in the open.
2. **Communicate:** Call out enemy positions to your team.
3. **Economy Management:** Don't force-buy every round. Coordinate eco rounds.
4. **Ability Timing:** Save abilities for key moments, don't waste them.
5. **Crosshair Placement:** Keep your crosshair at head level.

### Team Composition

A balanced team typically includes:
- 1 Tank (Vanta)
- 1-2 DPS (Katana, Zero)
- 1 Support/Utility (Mira, Glyph)
- 1 Controller (Byte, Spark)

### Map Awareness

- Learn common angles and peek spots
- Use audio cues to track enemy positions
- Control key choke points with area-denial abilities

### Hero Synergies

| Combo | Effect |
|-------|--------|
| Vanta + Mira | Unkillable frontline with heals |
| Glyph + Katana | Intel leads to assassination |
| Byte + Spark | Total area lockdown |
| Spectre + Zero | Flanking intel for sniper picks |
